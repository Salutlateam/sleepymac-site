FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I install SleepyMac?+

1. Download the .dmg file from the button above. 2. Open the DMG and drag SleepyMac into the Applications folder. 3. On first launch, right-click → Open (macOS asks for confirmation since the app isn't from the App Store). 4. The app will ask for your license key — you can buy one from the Pricing section.

What version of macOS is required?+

SleepyMac requires macOS 13 Ventura or later. Natively optimized for Apple Silicon (M1–M4) and compatible with Intel.

Can I cancel at any time?+

Yes, cancel in one click from your Lemon Squeezy dashboard. Access remains active until the end of your current paid period.

Does the MagSafe LED work on every Mac?+

The LED feature requires the batt utility — SleepyMac will offer to install it automatically on first use. Compatible with Apple Silicon MacBook Pro with MagSafe.

Does SleepyMac slow down my Mac?+

No. When no timer is active, CPU usage is 0%. The app uses less than 1 MB of RAM. Zero performance impact.

Does SleepyMac access the internet?+

Only to validate your license via Lemon Squeezy (once per day). The timer itself runs 100% locally, with no network connection. Your data never leaves your Mac.

What's the difference between Yearly and Lifetime?+

Yearly charges €12.99 each year (cancelable). Lifetime is a one-time payment of €24.99 — you own the app forever with all future updates, no recurring fees.
